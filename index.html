I wish to have no connection with any ship that does not sail fast, for I intend to go in harm's way.


John Paul Jones was the United States' first well-known naval commander in the American Revolutionary War. His actions in British waters during the Revolution earned him an international reputation that persists to this day. This earned him the nickname "Father of the American Navy."
